Smart Window & Heating Advisor

Make better heating decisions with real-time indoor intelligence.

Many households waste energy by heating rooms while windows are open, often without realising the impact on comfort and energy efficiency. Eye Opener is an intelligent indoor monitoring system that helps users understand their environment and make informed decisions about ventilation and heating.

The Problem

Heating systems are typically operated without real-time feedback on indoor conditions.

01

Unnecessary heat loss

Heat escapes quickly through open windows, wasting energy.

02

Increased energy consumption

More heating is required to maintain comfort.

03

Reduced indoor comfort

Temperature swings affect wellbeing and focus.

Without clear and timely information, it is difficult for occupants to balance ventilation and energy efficiency.

Our Solution

Eye Opener continuously monitors indoor environmental conditions and provides clear, actionable recommendations to the user.

By combining sensor data with simple decision logic, the system advises when it is more energy-efficient to open a window or turn off the heating transforming raw data into meaningful guidance.

How It Works
Step 01

Sensors collect indoor environmental data.

Step 02

Data is transmitted wirelessly to a central system.

Step 03

Decision logic evaluates energy efficiency conditions.

Step 04

Recommendations are displayed on a web interface in real time.
